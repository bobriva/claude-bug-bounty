# Business Logic Knowledge

## Definition

Business Logic Vulnerabilities occur when application behavior can be manipulated to achieve unintended outcomes while using legitimate functionality.

## Common Root Causes

- Trusting user behavior
- Missing validation
- Workflow assumptions
- State assumptions
- Domain misunderstandings
- Client-side trust

## Why They Matter

Business Logic flaws frequently:

- Evade scanners
- Evade automated testing
- Produce high business impact
- Lead to critical findings

## Mindset

The goal is not to break the application.

The goal is to use the application exactly as intended, but in a sequence or manner developers never anticipated.

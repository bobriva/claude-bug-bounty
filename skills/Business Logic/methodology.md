# Business Logic Methodology

## Phase 1 - Understand the Business

Determine:

- What is the purpose?
- What generates revenue?
- What creates cost?
- What grants access?
- What grants privileges?

---

## Phase 2 - Map Workflows

Document:

Registration

Login

Checkout

Payment

Refund

Reward

Promotion

Approval

Subscription

Cancellation

---

## Phase 3 - Identify Assumptions

Ask:

What does the developer assume?

Examples:

- User follows workflow
- User enters valid values
- User completes previous steps
- User owns resource
- User acts honestly

---

## Phase 4 - Violate Assumptions

Try:

- Skip steps
- Repeat steps
- Reorder steps
- Remove parameters
- Modify values
- Replay requests

---

## Phase 5 - Evaluate Impact

Can attacker:

- Gain money
- Gain access
- Gain privileges
- Cause business loss
- Cause fraud

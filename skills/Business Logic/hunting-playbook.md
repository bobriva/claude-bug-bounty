# Business Logic Hunting Playbook

Question every assumption.

For every workflow ask:

1. Can I skip it?
2. Can I repeat it?
3. Can I reverse it?
4. Can I perform it twice?
5. Can I perform it out of order?
6. Can I modify hidden values?
7. Can I remove parameters?
8. Can I use unexpected values?
9. Can I trigger concurrent actions?
10. Can I combine unrelated features?

Always look for:

Money

Access

Privileges

Business Impact

Fraud
